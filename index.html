Our Vision

We want to allow people to listen to the newest songs or albums from their favorite artist. We want people to be able to make their own playlists for whatever the occasion is. Be able to listen to whatever radio station they prefer wherever they are. Cheers to a new way to get legal music before your friends.

Features

Create various types of playlists that its to your music needs

Listen to different radio stations from around the world, either from the comfort of your home or when you are on the go

Listen to your favourite artists new songs or albums before the release date

We’ll help you make your music library filled with the latest songs you want to listen too. You can even collect albums and songs from an iPad or laptop. And importing an existing playlist into Music Bar is a snap, no matter how big or small it is.
